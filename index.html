Overview

The quality control of Printed Circuit Board Assemblies (PCBA) is a cornerstone of modern manufacturing. However, current Industrial AI faces a bottleneck: the lack of Cross-Scenario Generalization and Fine-Grained Severity Grading.

IDA 2026 invites researchers to solve two critical problems: adapting models to unseen production lines (Track 1) and intelligently assessing defect risk levels (Track 2).

Track 1: Detection

Focuses on Adaptability. Detect and localize defects in unseen production lines (Zero-Shot/Few-Shot).

Track 2: Grading

Focuses on Depth. Analyze defect mechanism and assign severity: Acceptable, Marginal NG, NG, or Gross NG.

Evaluation Criteria

Track 1: Detection Score

Emphasizes minimizing false alarms in unseen domains.

$$ S_{Track1} = 0.3 \times S_{loc} + 0.3 \times S_{cls} + 0.4 \times S_{screen} $$

Track 2: Risk Assessment Score

Emphasizes the accuracy of severity grading (Quadratic Weighted Kappa).

$$ S_{Track2} = 0.2 \times S_{loc} + 0.2 \times S_{cls} + 0.6 \times S_{grade} $$
